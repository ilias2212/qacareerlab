QA Career Lab Automation — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

QA Career Lab Automation ("the App") is a personal automation tool used to publish content to TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube on behalf of the QA Career Lab account. This privacy policy explains how we handle data.

2. Data We Collect

The App accesses the following data solely to publish content:

  • TikTok account ID and username
  • Access tokens required to post videos on your behalf

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users or viewers.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API
  • Upload and publish video content to the @QACareerLab TikTok account

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally and used only for publishing. No user data is retained beyond what is required for authentication.
